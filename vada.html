VADA

VADA, or (Visual Arts and Design Academy) is a program offered by SBHS that offers art classes to any student from 9th grade to 12th grade that will grow and challenge your artistic abilities. Including the art classes, VADA students also take general education classes that follow College Prep or an Advanced Placement pathways. Specifically:

    VADA Classes
  • 9th / Freshman: VADA Freehand Drawing
  • 10th / Sophomore: VADA Freehand Drawing Advanced / Intro to Graphic Design (SBCC Dual)
  • 11th / Junior: VADA Advanced Painting / Digital Imaging I (SBCC Dual)
  • 12th / Senior: VADA AP Studio Art or Honors Studio Art
      • College Prep Classes
  • 9th / Freshman: English 9 CP
  • 10th / Sophomore: English 10 CP / World History CP / Biology CP
  • 11th / Junior: English 11 / US History / Marine Biology
  • 12th / Senior: American Government / Economics
      • Advanced Placement Classes
  • 9th / Freshman: English 9 Honors
  • 10th / Sophomore: English 10 Honors / AP World History / Honors Chemistry
  • 11th / Junior: AP English Language / AP US History / AP Environmental Science
  • 12th / Senior: AP English Literature
