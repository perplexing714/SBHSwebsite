Advanced Placement (AP)

AP classes are college-level classes that are offered to students if they'd like a bigger challenge. They have the possibility to give college credit and an advantage in the application processes. Here at SBHS, we offer the following AP classes:

  • AP Biology
  • AP Calculus AB
  • AP Calculus BC
  • AP Chemistry
  • AP Computer Science A
  • AP English Language and Composition
  • AP English Literature and Composition
  • AP Environmental Science
  • AP Human Geography
  • AP Macroeconomics
  • AP Precalculus
  • AP Physics 1
  • AP Physics 2
  • AP Seminar
  • AP Spanish Language and Culture
  • AP Spanish Literature and Culture
  • AP Statistics
  • AP Studio Art: 2D Design
  • AP Studio Art: Drawing
  • AP United States Government and Politics
