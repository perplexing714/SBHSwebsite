MAD Academy

The MAD Academy (Multimedia Arts and Design Academy) is a program that SBHS offers to any student 9th grade - 12th grade. It helps students that are interested in exploring a career in media arts. It offers classes in filmmaking, photography, graphic design and web design. MAD is a SBCC dual enrollment program that holds students up to college level academic and professional level standards.

Electives

  • 9th Grade / Freshman: Intro to Media Arts / Social Media - Web Design
  • 10th Grade / Sophomore: Digital Imaging / Digital Drawing
  • 11th Grade / Junior: Non Linear Editing / Film Production
  • 12th Grade / Senior: Photography / Photojournalism / Advanced Film Production

Academics

  • 9th Grade / Freshman: MAD English 9 (honors or college prep)
  • 10th Grade / Sophomore: MAD English 10 (honors or college prep) / MAD World History (AP or college prep) / MAD Chemistry (honors or college prep)
  • 11th Grade / Junior: MAD English 11 or AP ELA / MAD US History (AP or college prep) / MAD Enviromental Science AP or MAD Marine Biology (college prep)
  • 12th Grade / Senior: POLS 101 - MAD Economics
